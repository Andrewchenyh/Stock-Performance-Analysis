# Overview

Analyzes U.S. Big Tech stocks (AAPL, AMZN, GOOG) vs. the S&P 500 (SPY) from 2014–2024 and examines defensive sector ETFs (XLP, XLU, XLV) during the 2022 bear market. Focuses on returns, volatility, drawdowns, and portfolio risk management.

# Key Findings

Big Tech massively outperformed SPY over 10 years but had larger drawdowns.

2022 bear market: AMZN –50%, GOOG –43.6%, AAPL –30.35%, SPY –18.65%

Defensive sectors showed smaller declines, providing stability and reducing panic selling.

# Methods

S&P 500 data scraped from Wikipedia

Historical prices via yfinance

Calculated daily/cumulative returns and max drawdowns

Visualized with matplotlib
